About Me

My name is Nate Bordeau, and I'm a senior at SUNY Plattsburgh. In May 2024, I will graduate with a double-major in Television Video Production and Digital Media Production, as well as a minor in English. I have done various kinds of work in production, from working with Columbia University, to the North Star Underground Railroad Museum. My goal is to write and produce content in the television and movie industry.
